About Us
PT. Bintang Selamanya is a leading agricultural company based in Kuala Tungkal, Indonesia, specializing in the export of high-quality bettlenut to countries such as Pakistan, India, and Bangladesh. With over 12 years of experience in the industry, our company has gained a reputation for being one of the biggest producers of bettlenut in the region.
Established in 2008, PT. Bintang Selamanya has been committed to providing premium quality bettlenut to our clients around the world. Our team of experts has extensive knowledge and expertise in cultivating and processing bettlenut, ensuring that every batch meets international standards.
PT. Bintang Selamanya stands firm in its commitment towards providing the best products and services to our clients. We are continuously exploring new opportunities for growth and expansion while also maintaining our position as a market leader in the bettlenut industry. Our goal is not only to meet but exceed the expectations of our customers, making us their preferred choice for all their bettlenut needs.
In conclusion, PT. Bintang Selamanya takes great pride in being a reputable company that values quality, sustainability, and social responsibility. With an unwavering focus on customer satisfaction and continuous improvement, we look forward to serving our valued clients around the world for many years to come.
